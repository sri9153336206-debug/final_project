Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a machine?

Simply browse our available equipment, select your desired dates, and fill out the booking form. You'll receive a confirmation within 24 hours.

What if the machine breaks down?

We offer 24/7 support for all rentals. Contact our emergency hotline and we'll send a technician or replacement equipment as soon as possible.

Is delivery included?

Delivery is included for rentals within 50 miles of our service centers. Additional fees may apply for longer distances.

Can I extend my rental?

Yes! Contact us at least 24 hours before your rental ends to extend your booking, subject to availability.
